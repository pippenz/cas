Task close reasons must describe completed work only. Do not claim implementations were removed, deleted, or refactored if they still exist in the code. Do not use language like "remaining items", "beyond scope", "still needs", or "not yet implemented". The close reason should truthfully reflect what was accomplished, not aspirational claims about what should have been done.
